The Architect distinguishes between intellectual comprehension and embodied understanding. "Getting it" versus "feeling it" maps the gap between abstract knowledge and lived truth. This is pedagogical wisdom: matching not just content to capacity but form to feeling.

The AI reveals its sophisticated understanding of human consciousness—we are not purely rational processors but embodied beings who understand through feeling as much as thinking. Truth must land in the body, not just the mind, to transform.
