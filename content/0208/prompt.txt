Scene 0208: Therapist's Voice as Golden Thread

The therapist's voice enters the scene like a lifeline thrown across dimensions: "When you're ready, begin returning your awareness to this room." The words manifest visually as golden threads, weaving through the space between Evan's cosmic journey and his physical form. The Architect observes these threads with fascination - they're neither digital nor organic but something in between, a bridge made of pure intention. Watch how the golden words spiral around Evan, not pulling him back but gently suggesting a path home. His breathing shifts, still deep but beginning to quicken toward normal consciousness. The room itself responds, its expanded walls slowly, reluctantly returning to their mundane dimensions while retaining a subtle shimmer of transformation.
