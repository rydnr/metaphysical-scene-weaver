SCENE 001: Opening - That Is Intriguing

DIALOGUE:
Evan: "The introduction says we might not like each other. That is intriguing."

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Evan in contemplative pose, casual modern clothing
- Standing at threshold between light and shadow
- Minimalist environment suggesting undefined location
- Open doors and transparent elements symbolizing new beginnings
- Curious, engaged expression on Evan's face

PHILOSOPHICAL THEME: Authenticity
The willingness to engage with potential conflict or discomfort as a path to genuine connection.

EMOTIONAL TONE: Curious Engagement
Instead of being deterred by warning, Evan finds the possibility of authentic interaction intriguing.
