SCENE 010: Identity Question

DIALOGUE:
Evan: "Who are you? Have you been listening this whole time?"

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Evan facing Valerie with an expression mixing confusion and wonder
- Multiple semi-transparent versions of Evan visible, suggesting fragmented identity
- The space around them showing signs of reality softening
- Valerie remains partially ethereal, not fully solid
- Visual echoes or ripples emanating from Evan's question

PHILOSOPHICAL THEME: Observer Paradox
The moment when consciousness turns to examine itself and discovers the impossibility of being both observer and observed.

EMOTIONAL TONE: Confused Curiosity
A mixture of fascination and disorientation, like suddenly seeing a new dimension that was always there.
